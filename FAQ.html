Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an account on the hotel reviewing website?

    To create an account on our website, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form with your details.

  • What should I include in my hotel review?

    Asking your self question such as are the rooms clean and comfortable?how about their quality?was I satisfied by their services?were the value i paid for them fair and worth it? should help you to share your experiences and opinions in a better way.

  • Can I leave a review for a hotel without creating an account?

    No, you need to have an account and be logged in to leave a review for a hotel.

  • How do I search for hotels on the website?

    To search for hotels on our website, simply enter your destination and dates in the search bar on the homepage and click "Search". You can also use the filters to narrow down your search results.

  • Can i see photos of the hotel in the website?

    Yes. Pictures of the hotel are included in our website.

  • Can I leave a review anonymously?

    Of course!We prioritize your privacy.You have the option to leave an anonymous review.

  • Can i edit or delete my review after submitting it?

    Yes. You can edit and delete your review whenever you want.

  • How can I contact hotels through the website?

    Contact information about the hotels such as phone number,website and locations are included in our website. You can contact them using these informations.
